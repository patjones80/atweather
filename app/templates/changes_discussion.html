With the current version of AT Weather - a couple of changes are made that are visible. Most obvious is that the background color is different. Less obvious is that the dropdown boxes no longer retain a "memory" of what you picked. Please note that the boxes now reset themselves after a forecast has been selected. This is not an error, but rather a means of simplifying how the website works "under the hood". This feature may return if a simpler means can be found of implementing it.

Also of note is that starting March 7th, 2017, AT Weather will use a new method for retrieving forecasts from the National Weather Service. Called an "API", it will not result in any significant change to how the forecasts are displayed, but it will result in some changes to their content. Most importantly is that hazardous weather headlines (taking the form of links that say, for example, "Severe Thunderstorm Watch" or "Winter Storm Warning") will be missing from the forecast text. If you're used to clicking on those links (when present), and getting redirected to the National Weather Service website where you can read the full text of the headline, this feature will no longer be present. This is an issue that will hopefully be resolved.

Thanks so much for using AT Weather. As always, feel free to contact me with questions about the site or weather in general!
